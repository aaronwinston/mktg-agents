## TARGETING STRATEGY

### Audience 1: Lookalike (40% of budget)
**Seed:** Existing Arize customer list + past Observe attendees  
**Message:** Variant 2 (Authority) or Variant 3 (FOMO)  
**Why:** Warm audience; peer authority and social proof drive conversion

### Audience 2: Retargeting Site Visitors (35% of budget)
**Pixel:** Anyone who visited observe.arize.com in last 30 days but didn't convert  
**Message:** Variant 1 (Problem) or Variant 4 (Specificity)  
**Why:** They showed intent; need a different angle to push them over the line

### Audience 3: AI Engineering Job Titles (25% of budget)
**LinkedIn targeting:** Job titles containing "AI Engineer," "ML Engineer," "Head of AI," "AI Lead," "ML Ops," "Agent Developer"  
**Message:** Variant 4 (Specificity) or Variant 1 (Problem)  
**Why:** Cold audience; specific problem + framework = higher conversion than generic "conference promo"

## DESIGN NOTES

All creative should maintain:
- **Aesthetic:** Technical, minimal, professional (not marketing-y)
- **Color:** Arize brand primary + white space
- **Tone:** Problem-first, specific, builder-focused
- **Copy length:** Short sentences; two-part contrast; avoid marketing language
- **Imagery:** Real screenshots, flow diagrams, or authentic people (avoid stock photos of "teamwork")
