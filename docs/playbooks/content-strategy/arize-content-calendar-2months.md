## DISTRIBUTION & REPURPOSING STRATEGY

### Each Long-Form Article Becomes:
- ✍️ 1 main article (3-5K words) → Published on company blog
- 🐦 1 Twitter/X thread (8-15 tweets) → Distribute on your personal account
- 📰 1-2 social clips (short excerpts) → LinkedIn, etc.
- 💬 1 discussion post → Dev.to, Hacker News, relevant communities
- 🎥 (Optional) 1 short video summary → YouTube/Loom clip

### Content Pyramid:
```
         [Thought Leadership Essay]
              (5K words)
                  ↓
    [Twitter Thread + Social Clips]
         (10-15 tweets + graphics)
                  ↓
      [Discussion Posts + Comments]
        (Dev communities + forums)
                  ↓
      [Organic Reach + Backlinks]
```

## EDITORIAL PRINCIPLES (ForgeOS-Aligned)

✅ **Workflow-First:** Every piece teaches a workflow or decision framework
✅ **Product-True:** Grounded in real Arize capabilities and customer use cases
✅ **Dev-Fluent:** Technical enough for engineers; accessible enough for product leaders
✅ **Low-Hype:** Avoids buzzwords; focuses on substance and operations
✅ **Reviewable Artifacts:** Links to docs, code, dashboards, case studies where possible
