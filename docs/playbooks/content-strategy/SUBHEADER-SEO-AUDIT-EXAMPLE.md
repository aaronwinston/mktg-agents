## SEO BEST PRACTICES APPLIED

✅ **Power Words:** Essential, Three, Master, Build, Organize  
✅ **Keyword Placement:** First/early in header (ranks better)  
✅ **Length:** 5-10 words (scannable, good for SERP snippets)  
✅ **Specificity:** No vague terms like "important," "possible," "interesting"  
✅ **Clarity:** Immediately clear what section covers  
✅ **Structure:** Question or declarative (not fragmented)
