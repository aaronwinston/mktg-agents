# Developer Campaign Quality Rubric

Use this rubric to score developer-facing campaigns, ads, landing pages, product videos, and launch assets.

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

---

## 1. Workflow clarity

### 5
The workflow is obvious in seconds. The asset shows the problem, action, and outcome.

### 3
The workflow is present but requires interpretation.

### 1
The asset lists features or benefits without showing what the developer does.

---

## 2. Product truth

### 5
Every claim is directly supported by product behavior, docs, screenshots, or approved messaging.

### 3
Most claims are accurate, but some wording is loose.

### 1
The asset implies unsupported behavior or overstates capability.

---

## 3. Artifact clarity

### 5
The output is concrete: diff, PR, test result, deployment, trace, report, issue, dashboard, etc.

### 3
The outcome is implied but not visible.

### 1
The asset ends on a vague benefit.

---

## 4. Developer fluency

### 5
The language sounds like a competent developer or technical PM wrote it.

### 3
Some phrases are dev-fluent, but the asset still feels market-y.

### 1
The copy uses generic AI/productivity language.

---

## 5. Trust and reviewability

### 5
The asset clearly shows how the developer remains in control through review, tests, checks, approvals, or policies.

### 3
Reviewability is mentioned but not central.

### 1
The asset implies autonomous output without human review.

---

## 6. Message continuity

### 5
Ad, landing page, CTA, and product UI tell the same story.

### 3
The story mostly aligns but some elements drift.

### 1
The ad promise and landing page experience do not match.

---

## 7. Conversion quality

### 5
The asset attracts the right audience, even if it filters out others.

### 3
The asset is broadly appealing but less targeted.

### 1
The asset optimizes for clicks from the wrong audience.

---

## Interpretation

### 31–35
Excellent. Ready to ship.

### 25–30
Strong. Tighten specific weak spots.

### 18–24
Needs revision. Workflow or trust boundary is unclear.

### Below 18
Not ready. Rebuild from product truth.
