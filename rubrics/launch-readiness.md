# Launch readiness rubric

## Score definitions

### Score 5 (Excellent)
All assets are reviewed and approved. Product, technical, and claims reviews are complete. Executive sign-off is confirmed. All formats are ready. Launch timing is confirmed. Distribution plan is complete.

### Score 3 (Acceptable)
Core assets are ready but some variants are missing. Most reviews are complete. Timing is confirmed.

### Score 1 (Poor)
Key assets are missing or unreviewed. Claims are not cleared. No distribution plan. Not ready to launch.

## Reviewer questions

- Are all required assets complete?
- Have product, technical, and claims reviews been completed?
- Is executive sign-off confirmed?
- Is launch timing confirmed and communicated?
- Is the distribution plan ready for each channel?

## Common failure modes

- Missing required asset variants
- Unreviewed product or benchmark claims
- No executive sign-off
- Launch timing not confirmed
- Distribution plan incomplete
