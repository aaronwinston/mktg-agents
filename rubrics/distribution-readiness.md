# Distribution readiness rubric

## Score definitions

### Score 5 (Excellent)
The asset has a complete distribution plan: primary channel, secondary channels, CTA, social variants, internal stakeholders, timing, and success metrics.

### Score 3 (Acceptable)
The asset has a primary channel and CTA but no social variants or secondary channels defined.

### Score 1 (Poor)
No distribution plan. No CTA. No social variants. Not ready to publish.

## Reviewer questions

- Is the primary channel defined?
- Is the CTA clear and functional?
- Are social variants ready?
- Are internal stakeholders notified?
- Is launch timing confirmed?

## Common failure modes

- No CTA
- No social variants
- No internal notification plan
- Missing timing or embargo information
- No success metric defined
