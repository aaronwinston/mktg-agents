# Excellent launch example

*An example of strong launch copy for a developer-facing product feature.*

---

## Announcement: Trace comparison is now in Arize

You can now compare two traces side by side.

Select any two traces in your project and see exactly where they diverge: which steps produced different outputs, where latency changed, which tool calls behaved differently.

This matters most when you are trying to understand what changed between a run that worked and one that did not. Before this, that comparison required manual inspection across separate views. Now it is one click.

---

## Blog intro

Debugging an agent often starts with a question: what is different between this run and the last one?

The model is the same. The prompt is the same. Or so you think. Something changed, and the output changed with it.

Trace comparison gives you a direct answer. Select two traces, and see a side-by-side view of every step, every input, and every output. The differences are highlighted automatically.

Teams use this for three things: debugging unexpected behavior, verifying that a fix worked, and understanding what changed after a model or prompt update.

---

## What makes this strong

- Leads with what changed, not with enthusiasm
- Second sentence explains the specific capability
- Third sentence explains the use case (when this matters)
- Blog intro opens with the reader's problem, not the product
- No hype language
- Claims are specific and verifiable
