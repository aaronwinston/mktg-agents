# Weekly editorial workflow

## Frequency
Every Monday.

## Steps
1. Review active briefs and in-progress content.
2. Run `skills/editorial/managing-editor/SKILL.md` to assess priorities.
3. Identify upcoming deadlines and blockers.
4. Confirm owners for each active asset.
5. Update the editorial calendar.
6. Brief the team on the week's priorities.
7. Run `skills/foundation/ai-researcher/SKILL.md` for weekly research digest.

## Output
- Updated editorial calendar
- Confirmed owners and deadlines
- Weekly research digest
- Identified blockers
