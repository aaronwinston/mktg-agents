# Launch day workflow

## Trigger
Launch date confirmed by product.

## Pre-launch (T-5 to T-2 days)
1. Confirm all assets are complete.
2. Run `skills/quality/final-publish-reviewer/SKILL.md` on all assets.
3. Confirm executive sign-off.
4. Confirm all claims approvals.
5. Brief internal stakeholders.

## Launch day (T-0)
1. Publish blog post.
2. Send email announcement.
3. Post social content (company account first, then executive).
4. Notify sales and partner teams.
5. Monitor for questions or corrections.

## Post-launch (T+1 to T+7)
1. Review performance metrics.
2. Respond to developer questions.
3. Repurpose for newsletter and secondary channels.
4. Update `tests/evaluation-notes.md` with lessons.
