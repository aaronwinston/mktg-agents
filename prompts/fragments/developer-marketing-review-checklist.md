# Developer Marketing Review Checklist

Use this prompt fragment when reviewing developer-facing content.

Ask:

1. What real developer problem does this address?
2. What exact workflow does the asset show?
3. What product surface does the workflow happen in?
4. What artifact does the developer get?
5. Where does review happen?
6. Where do tests, checks, approvals, or policies appear?
7. Does the ad copy match the product UI?
8. Does the landing page continue the same story?
9. Are there any unsupported autonomy, continuity, scale, or security claims?
10. Does the asset teach something concrete?

Default verdict:

If the asset does not show a workflow, ask for the workflow before rewriting.
