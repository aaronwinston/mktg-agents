## Package Overview

The Social Campaign Package delivers a coordinated multi-platform social media campaign with native optimized content for each channel. It bundles graphics, video, copy, and engagement strategy to drive awareness, community growth, and traffic.

**Business Objective:**
- Build awareness on target topics
- Grow engaged community/follower base
- Drive traffic to owned assets
- Generate conversations and brand mentions

**Target Audience:** Industry professionals, practitioners, decision-makers (varies by platform)

**Distribution Channels:** LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook (native optimized)

**Expected Impact:** 
- 100K+ total impressions
- 3-5% engagement rate
- 10%+ follower growth
- 2%+ click-through rate

## Content Components

### 1. LinkedIn Campaign (Primary Professional Channel)

#### Content Quantity & Cadence
- 15-20 posts over 4-6 weeks
- 3x per week posting schedule
- Tuesday-Thursday 8am-1pm optimal timing
- Mix of content types (text, image, document, article)

#### Post Types & Specifications

**Thought Leadership Posts** (3-4 posts)
- Format: Text post with thought-provoking statement
- Length: 200-300 characters (auto-truncated at ~175 chars)
- Content: Industry insight, contrarian take, relevant hot topic
- Image: Professional photo or brand graphic
- CTA: "What's your take?" or "Agree/Disagree?"

**Educational/How-To Posts** (4-5 posts)
- Format: Carousel document (3-5 slides) or text post
- Content: Step-by-step guide, best practices, tips
- Slides: Numbered steps, visual callouts, data
- CTA: "Save this for reference"

**Data/Stats Posts** (3-4 posts)
- Format: Quote graphic or carousel
- Content: Industry statistic, research finding, benchmark data
- Design: Branded graphic with single stat + context
- CTA: Link to full research/article

**Employee/Team Posts** (2-3 posts)
- Format: Image + text
- Content: Spotlight on team member, career insights, behind-scenes
- Image: Professional photo
- Purpose: Humanize brand, build culture visibility

**Content Repurposing** (2-3 posts)
- Format: LinkedIn article (republish blog post)
- Content: Full article from blog
- CTA: "Read the full analysis"
- Distribution: Mixed LinkedIn article + announcement post

**Engagement Posts** (2-3 posts)
- Format: Question or poll
- Content: Ask for audience input, create conversation
- CTA: Comments encouraged
- Follow-up: Like and reply to comments

#### Asset Specifications
- Featured image: 1200x627px minimum
- Carousel: 1200x627px per slide
- Document: Vertical format, readable at small size
- Color: Brand-aligned, readable on LinkedIn
- Text overlay: Minimal on images (LinkedIn penalizes)

### 2. Twitter/X Campaign

#### Content Quantity & Cadence
- 20-25 tweets over 4-6 weeks
- Daily or near-daily posting
- 9am-1pm optimal posting windows
- Mix of standalone tweets and threads

#### Tweet Types

**Announcement Threads** (1-2 threads, 5-8 tweets each)
- Format: Connected tweet thread
- Content: Major insight broken into sequential tweets
- First tweet: Hook that stops scroll
- Middle tweets: Supporting points, data, examples
- Last tweet: CTA (retweet, link, reply)

**Quick Tips** (4-5 tweets)
- Format: Standalone tweet with image or emoji
- Length: 200-250 characters
- Content: Practical advice, best practice, warning
- Image: Quote graphic or illustration
- CTA: "Save this" or "Retweet if you agree"

**Stats/Data Tweets** (3-4 tweets)
- Format: Text with stat + image
- Content: Single statistic with context
- Image: Chart, graph, or infographic
- CTA: Link to source/article

**Conversation Starters** (4-5 tweets)
- Format: Question or controversial take
- Length: 150-200 characters
- Content: Prompt discussion on relevant topic
- CTA: "What's your experience?"

**Retweet & Engage** (Ongoing)
- Monitor relevant conversations
- Retweet with added value commentary
- Reply to relevant accounts
- Join trending conversations (brand-appropriate)

#### Asset Specifications
- Images: 1024x512px or 16:9 ratio
- Video: 15-60 seconds, vertical or square
- Text: No link shorteners (use full URLs)
- Hashtags: 1-3 relevant hashtags
- @ mentions: Tag relevant accounts where appropriate

### 3. Instagram Campaign

#### Content Quantity & Cadence
- 8-12 feed posts over 4-6 weeks
- 2-3 posts per week
- Stories: 5-7 frame stories 3-4 times per week
- Reels: 2-4 short videos (15-60 sec)

#### Feed Post Types

**Hero/Brand Posts** (2-3 posts)
- Format: High-quality image or carousel
- Content: Product/service showcase, brand moment
- Image: Professional photography, 1080x1350px
- Caption: 100-150 words with hashtags
- CTA: Bio link, DM, or engagement

**Educational Carousel** (2-3 posts)
- Format: 5-7 slide carousel
- Content: Tips, how-to, behind-the-scenes
- Slides: Vertical format, readable text, visuals
- Caption: Introductory text + CTA

**User-Generated Content/Testimonial** (1-2 posts)
- Format: Customer photo or quote graphic
- Content: Customer spotlight, success story
- Caption: Customer story (100-150 words)
- Credit: Tag customer/partner
- CTA: "Share your story"

**Motivational/Inspirational** (1-2 posts)
- Format: Quote graphic or image
- Content: Industry insight, leadership quote
- Design: Branded quote graphic
- Caption: Context or explanation
- CTA: "Save this" or "Share with your team"

**Reels** (2-4 videos)
- Format: 15-60 second videos
- Content: Product demo, quick tips, trending audio
- Specs: Vertical (1080x1920px), captions, music
- CTA: Bio link, swipe up (if eligible), DM

#### Story Content (Daily/3-4x/week)
- Tip of the day
- Behind-the-scenes moments
- Polls and questions
- Countdown to event/announcement
- Team highlights
- Quick educational content (repurposed)

#### Hashtag Strategy
- 15-30 hashtags per post
- Mix: 5 branded, 10 niche, 15 broad/popular
- Place in first comment (Instagram algorithm)
- Examples: #MarketingTips #SocialStrategy #ContentMarketing
- Brand hashtag: Create and promote campaign hashtag

#### Asset Specifications
- Feed images: 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- Carousel: 1080x1350px per slide
- Stories: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- Reels: 1080x1920px, vertical
- Video format: MP4 or MOV
- Captions: All video must have captions
- Color: Brand palette, visually cohesive feed

### 4. TikTok Campaign (Optional but Growing Audience)

#### Content Quantity & Cadence
- 4-8 short videos over 4-6 weeks
- 1 video per week or 2x weekly
- Format: 15-60 seconds, vertical

#### Video Types

**Product/Service Demo** (1-2 videos)
- Format: Fast-paced walkthrough
- Content: Feature demo, benefit highlight
- Audio: Trending TikTok audio (where appropriate)
- CTA: Bio link, comment with question

**Educational Tips** (2-3 videos)
- Format: Quick tip or how-to
- Content: Industry insight, best practice
- Text overlays: Key points
- Captions: All dialogue captioned
- Audio: Trending or custom music

**Behind-the-Scenes** (1-2 videos)
- Format: Authentic, less polished
- Content: Team moment, office culture, day-in-life
- Vibe: Authentic, humanizing
- CTA: "Follow for more BTS content"

**Trend Participation** (1-2 videos)
- Format: Trending sound/format with brand twist
- Content: Adapt trending format to your topic
- Audio: Trending TikTok sound
- Hashtag: Use trending hashtags

#### Asset Specifications
- Resolution: 1080x1920px minimum
- Frame rate: 24-60 fps
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Length: 15-60 seconds optimal
- Captions: Burned-in or TikTok captions
- Music: Licensed TikTok sounds or own music
- No watermarks (other than brand watermark)

### 5. Supporting Assets

#### Graphics/Visual Templates
- Social post template (standard post)
- Quote graphic template
- Data/stat graphic template
- Video thumbnail template
- Story template pack (5+ variations)

#### Hashtag Library
- 5-10 branded hashtags
- 20-30 niche/industry hashtags
- 10-15 trending/broad hashtags
- Combination sets for different post types

#### Content Calendar
- 4-6 week calendar with:
  - Posted date, platform, time
  - Content type and topic
  - Author and status
  - Performance metrics (post-publication)

#### Caption Variations
- Multiple caption options per topic
- Short versions (100 chars)
- Long versions (2000 chars)
- CTAs: Different calls-to-action options
- Hashtag combinations: Pre-built sets

### 6. Engagement & Community Management

#### Response Plan
- Monitor comments and DMs
- Response time SLA: 24 hours for public comments
- Response templates for common questions
- Escalation path for complex inquiries
- Engagement goals:
  - Like 50%+ of relevant audience comments
  - Reply to 75%+ of direct questions
  - Retweet/share 2-3 relevant audience content per week

#### Community Building Activities
- Weekly engagement post (poll, question)
- 2-3 hours per week responding to comments
- Monthly community highlight (user spotlight)
- Hashtag monitoring for mentions
- Competitor monitoring (mentions, activity)

## Distribution Timeline

### Pre-Campaign (1 week before)
- Content calendar finalized
- All graphics designed and approved
- Captions written and reviewed
- Videos edited and optimized
- Publishing tools set up (Buffer, Later, etc.)
- Team trained on posting schedule
- Analytics baselines recorded

### Launch Week (Week 1)
- Announcement across all platforms
- Begin posting on schedule
- Daily monitoring of engagement
- Early adjustments to content if needed
- Community management active

### Weeks 2-4
- Maintain regular posting cadence
- Analyze performance weekly
- Engage with comments/replies
- Repurpose top-performing content
- Refine strategy based on early data

### Weeks 5-6
- Continue regular posting
- Begin highlighting top performers
- Consider paid amplification if budget available
- Wind-down planning (if campaign has end date)

### Coverage & Completeness
- [ ] 100% of scheduled content published

- [ ] Content calendar followed
