## Package Overview

The Analyst Report Package delivers an authoritative, data-driven research report that establishes thought leadership, generates qualified leads, and provides customer intelligence. It bundles a comprehensive report with strategic repurposing across multiple formats.

**Business Objective:**
- Establish authority on market topic
- Generate high-quality leads (email capture)
- Drive customer intelligence/insights
- Create credibility and trust

**Target Audience:** Enterprise buyers, C-suite, analysts, industry professionals

**Distribution Channels:** Gated download, email nurture, webinar, social promotion, PR

**Expected Impact:** 
- 500+ downloads (primary metric)
- 200+ email list adds
- 100+ webinar attendees
- 50+ sales conversations initiated

## Content Components

### 1. Core Asset: Full Research Report

**Format:** PDF (20-40 pages) + Web page  
**Structure:**
- Cover page: Title, date, author/company
- Executive summary (2-3 pages)
- Table of contents
- Introduction (2-3 pages): Problem, context, why it matters
- Methodology (1-2 pages): How the research was conducted
- Main findings (10-15 pages): Data, analysis, insights
  - 4-6 major sections, each with:
    - Data/statistics
    - Analysis and interpretation
    - Customer quotes or case examples
    - Visualizations (charts, graphs)
    - Key takeaway
- Recommendations (2-3 pages): What to do with findings
- Conclusion (1-2 pages): Future outlook
- Appendices: Detailed methodology, definitions, data tables
- Contact/resources page

**Specifications:**
- Professional design, branded
- Data visualizations (15-20 charts/graphs)
- Readable typography (11-14pt)
- Accessible (images have alt text, PDFs have structure)
- File size optimized for download
- Page numbers, headers/footers
- Clickable table of contents

### 2. Derivative Assets

#### Executive Summary
**Format:** PDF + web page (2-3 pages)  
**Content:**
- One-page version of full report
- Key findings bullets
- Main recommendation
- How to access full report
- CTA for next steps

**Use:** Quick reference, email-shareable, social proof

#### Data Sheets
**Format:** Visual PDFs (1-2 pages each, 3-4 sheets)  
**Content by topic:**
- Sheet 1: "Key Statistics" - Top 5-7 data points
- Sheet 2: "Market Trends" - 3-4 trends with data
- Sheet 3: "Best Practices" - Recommended approaches
- Sheet 4: "Competitive Analysis" - Key players overview

**Specs:**
- Infographic style
- Minimal text, maximum visuals
- Downloadable (not gated or gated separately)
- Social media optimized for sharing

#### Infographics
**Format:** PNG/SVG graphics (5-10 pieces)  
**Types:**
- Main key finding visual (large, shareable)
- Statistical comparison charts
- Trend visualization
- Methodology overview
- Recommended framework
- Top insights summary

**Specs:**
- Vertical or square (Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn)
- High resolution (300 dpi minimum)
- Social media optimized text/fonts
- Brand colors and styling
- Each 500x1000px or similar

### 3. Webinar/Presentation

**Format:** Live webinar or on-demand video (45-60 minutes)  
**Content:**
- Presenter introduction (3-5 min)
- Problem context/why this matters (5-7 min)
- Research methodology (2-3 min)
- Key findings walkthrough (20-25 min)
  - Present data, explain implications
  - Real-world examples
  - Customer quotes
- Recommendations and action steps (5-10 min)
- Demo or implementation example (5-10 min, optional)
- Q&A (10-15 min)
- Offer/CTA (2-3 min)

**Format:**
- Slide deck (40-50 slides)
- Screen recording with presenter
- Downloadable deck
- Chat engagement during live event
- Recorded for on-demand viewing

### 4. Landing Page

**Format:** Single-page conversion-focused HTML  
**Sections:**
- Hero: Report headline, value prop, image
- Why download: 3-5 reasons to download
- What's included: Preview of topics/chapters
- Key stats: 2-3 compelling data points from report
- Author/company credibility: Who conducted research
- Form: Email capture (name, email, company, role)
- Social proof: Customer logos or testimonials (if available)
- FAQ: 3-5 questions about report
- CTA: Download button (prominent)

**Specifications:**
- Mobile responsive, fast loading
- Form integrated with email platform
- Download confirmation email with link
- Follow-up email sequence triggered
- Analytics tracking configured
- A/B test versions (2-3 variations)

### 5. Email Campaign

#### Gate Notification Email
**Format:** Confirmation after download  
**Content:**
- Thank you for downloading
- Download link (if not auto-sent)
- Preview of key insights
- Invitation to webinar
- Contact for questions

#### Nurture Email Series (4-5 emails)
**Sequence (one email every 2-3 days):**
- Email 1: "Exclusive Finding #1" + webinar invitation
- Email 2: "Exclusive Finding #2" + customer example
- Email 3: "How Company X Used This Research" (case study)
- Email 4: "Action Plan Template" (bonus asset)
- Email 5: "Limited-Time Offer" (sales CTA or extended resource)

**Specifications:**
- Personalized (first name)
- Mobile responsive
- Varied CTAs (learn, download, attend, contact)
- Progressive disclosure of value

### 6. Social & PR Assets

#### Social Media Content (20-25 posts over 8 weeks)
**By platform:**

**LinkedIn:**
- Report announcement post (1000+ chars)
- Key finding posts (2-3 posts)
- Infographic posts (3-5 posts)
- Methodology/transparency post
- "Did you know?" stat posts (3-4)
- Webinar announcement (2-3 posts)
- Customer quote posts

**Twitter:**
- Report thread (5-7 tweets)
- Stat tweets (2x per week)
- Webinar promotion tweets
- Discussion-starting questions

**Other:**
- Facebook: Landing page promotion
- Medium: Full article republish
- SlideShare: Deck upload (if applicable)

#### Press Release
**Format:** Standard PR format (400-600 words)  
**Content:**
- Announcement: Research released
- Key finding: Most important discovery
- Why it matters: Context and impact
- Quotes: From authors/experts
- How to access: Download link, webinar info
- Company boilerplate
- Contact info

**Distribution:** 50-100 relevant outlets (PR Newswire or equivalent)

### 7. Supporting Assets

#### One-Pager Summary
**Format:** Visual PDF (1 page)  
**Content:**
- Report title and key stat
- 5-7 key findings visual
- 3-4 recommendations
- CTA (Download full report)
- Author/company info

#### Implementation Guide
**Format:** Bonus PDF for email subscribers (5-10 pages)  
**Content:**
- How to use findings in your organization
- Step-by-step action plan
- Resource allocation guide
- Success metrics
- Timeline recommendation

#### Customer Case Study
**Format:** 2-page PDF or blog post  
**Content:**
- Customer overview (industry, size)
- Challenge (what the research reveals)
- How they acted on findings
- Results (metrics)
- Lessons learned
- Quote from customer

## Distribution Timeline

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)
- Complete report final review
- Landing page setup and testing
- Email sequences configured
- Social calendar created
- Press release drafted
- Webinar technology tested

### Launch Week (Week 1)
- Day 1: Land page goes live (unlisted)
- Day 2: Soft announce to email list
- Day 3: Press release distribution
- Day 4: LinkedIn announcement
- Day 5: Webinar scheduled and promoted
- Day 7: Twitter thread posted

### Weeks 2-4
- Webinar #1 held (live)
- Continued email promotion to engaged users
- Daily/regular social posting
- Outreach to industry influencers
- Press coverage monitoring

### Weeks 5-8
- Webinar available on-demand
- Email nurture continues to new downloads
- Content repurposing (articles, videos)
- Advertorial or sponsored placement (optional)
- Case study interviews with customers
