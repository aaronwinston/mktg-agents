## Package Overview

The Repurposing Bundle transforms existing high-performing content into multiple formats and distributes them across new channels. It maximizes ROI on content investments and expands reach to new audiences without creating entirely new content.

**Business Objective:**
- Maximize ROI on existing content
- Expand reach to new channels and audiences
- Reduce content creation costs
- Accelerate time-to-market

**Target Audience:** Varies by format and channel, but often secondary/niche audiences compared to original

**Distribution Channels:** 5-8 new channels per piece (repurposing to different platforms)

**Expected Impact:** 
- 50K+ reach across all repurposed formats
- 70% cost savings vs. creating original content
- 80%+ successful format adaptation rate
- Audience reach extended by 3-5x original

## Content Components

### 1. Source Content Assessment

**Candidate Selection Criteria**
- Minimum 6 months published
- 100+ views or shares
- Topic remains relevant
- Positive sentiment/no controversial elements
- Not time-sensitive

**Typical High-Performing Sources**
- Blog posts (1500-2000 words)
- Whitepapers/reports (10-30 pages)
- Case studies (2-5 pages)
- Webinar recordings (30-60 minutes)
- Research findings or data

### 2. Format Adaptation Templates

#### Template 1: Blog Post → Slide Deck
**Transformation:**
- Original: 2000-word article
- Adapted: 15-20 slide presentation

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Title, author, date
- Slide 2: Agenda/outline
- Slides 3-15: One key point per slide (text + visuals)
- Slide 16: Key takeaways summary
- Slide 17-18: Resources, next steps, contact

**Specifications:**
- 16:9 format (standard widescreen)
- 1 main idea per slide
- Minimal text (bullet points max)
- 1 visual per slide (chart, image, icon)
- Readable from distance (24pt+ font)
- Branded colors and fonts
- Slide notes for presenter

**Use Cases:**
- Internal training
- Webinar presentation
- SlideShare upload
- Sales asset
- Conference talk

#### Template 2: Blog Post → Infographic
**Transformation:**
- Original: Article with multiple data points
- Adapted: 1-3 visual infographics

**Structure:**
- Title: Main topic/finding
- 3-5 key statistics visualized
- Icons and illustrations
- Color-coded sections
- Bottom: Source, date, company logo

**Specifications:**
- Vertical format preferred (1000x1500px)
- High contrast, readable colors
- Sans-serif fonts
- Data visualization best practices
- Social media optimized
- Mobile readable at small size

**Use Cases:**
- Social media sharing
- Pinterest
- LinkedIn documents
- Email headers
- Presentations
- Articles and blog posts

#### Template 3: Blog Post → Video Script
**Transformation:**
- Original: 2000-word article
- Adapted: 15-30 second social clip OR 3-5 minute YouTube video

**Script Structure (15-30 second version):**
- 0-2 sec: Hook ("Did you know?")
- 2-10 sec: Main point + supporting fact
- 10-20 sec: Why it matters
- 20-30 sec: CTA (link in bio, subscribe, etc.)
- Total words: 80-100

**Script Structure (3-5 minute version):**
- 0-30 sec: Hook and intro
- 30 sec-2 min: First key point with explanation
- 2-3 min: Second key point with example
- 3-4 min: Conclusion and recommendations
- 4-5 min: CTA and next steps

**Specifications:**
- Conversational tone (spoken, not written)
- Short sentences (10-15 words each)
- Include visual directions (B-roll, graphics, text overlays)
- On-screen text for captions
- Background music (royalty-free)
- Platform-specific framing (vertical, square, horizontal)

**Use Cases:**
- TikTok/Instagram Reels
- YouTube shorts
- LinkedIn video
- Email video preview
- Social media stories

#### Template 4: Blog Post → Social Media Thread
**Transformation:**
- Original: 2000-word article
- Adapted: 5-8 connected social posts

**Structure:**
- Tweet 1: Hook (curiosity, stat, question)
- Tweets 2-5: Key points (one per tweet, supporting data)
- Tweet 6: Insight or surprising conclusion
- Tweet 7: Call-to-action (link to full article)
- Tweet 8 (optional): How to apply this

**Specifications:**
- Each tweet stands alone but connects
- Include numbers/stats where applicable
- Use emojis strategically
- Hashtags in final tweet(s) only
- Include link to original content
- 1-2 hour spacing between tweets (if posting separately)

**Use Cases:**
- Twitter/X threads
- LinkedIn carousel posts
- Email newsletter series
- Blog post comments
- Forum discussions

#### Template 5: Blog Post → Email Series
**Transformation:**
- Original: 2000-word article
- Adapted: 3-4 email sequence

**Structure:**
- Email 1: "Introduction" - What you'll learn
  - Subject line: Teaser or question
  - Content: Hook + first key takeaway
  - CTA: "Read part 2"

- Email 2: "Deep Dive" - Main content
  - Subject line: Specific benefit or insight
  - Content: 2-3 key points with examples
  - CTA: "Continue to part 3"

- Email 3: "Application" - How to use this
  - Subject line: Action-oriented
  - Content: Practical steps, implementation guide
  - CTA: "Read the full article" or "Watch the webinar"

- Email 4 (optional): "Next Steps" - Related resources
  - Subject line: What's next
  - Content: Related topics, resources, recommendations
  - CTA: Product/service pitch or free resource

**Specifications:**
- Progressive disclosure (1 email every 1-2 days)
- Mobile responsive HTML
- Personalization tokens (first name, company)
- Consistent branding
- Clear, single focus per email
- 100-200 words per email (concise)

**Use Cases:**
- Email nurture campaigns
- Educational series
- Lead nurturing
- On-boarding sequences
- Re-engagement campaigns

#### Template 6: Blog Post → Interactive Asset
**Transformation:**
- Original: 2000-word article with data
- Adapted: Interactive tool, quiz, or calculator

**Examples:**
- Quiz: "What's your [topic] maturity level?" (5-8 questions)
- Calculator: Tool that computes based on inputs (ROI, savings, time)
- Assessment: Self-evaluation with results/recommendations
- Interactive timeline: Visual walkthrough of process/history
- Decision matrix: Help user choose between options

**Specifications:**
- Web-based (no app required)
- Mobile responsive
- 2-5 minutes to complete
- Results shareable or downloadable
- Leads captured (email address for results)
- Branded, matches site design

**Use Cases:**
- Lead generation
- User engagement
- Social sharing (results)
- SEO benefit (time on site)
- Email list growth

#### Template 7: Whitepaper → Case Study
**Transformation:**
- Original: 30-page research report
- Adapted: 2-3 page customer case study

**Structure:**
- Overview: Customer, industry, size
- Challenge: Problem the report identifies
- Solution: How they used the whitepaper insights
- Results: Metrics achieved
- Lessons: What they learned
- Quote: Testimonial from customer

**Specifications:**
- 2-3 pages PDF
- Similar design to whitepaper
- 50% text, 50% visuals/quotes
- Downloadable from landing page
- Shareable

**Use Cases:**
- Sales asset
- Social proof
- Lead generation
- Website content
- Case study library

## Distribution Timeline

### Preparation (Week 1)
- Identify candidate content (top 5-10 performers)
- Assess format feasibility for each
- Create detailed adaptation plan
- Assign ownership per format
- Gather source materials

### Adaptation (Weeks 2-3)
- Create all formats in parallel where possible
- Review and refine each adaptation
- QA and brand consistency checks
- Finalize all assets

### Distribution (Weeks 4-8, staggered)
- Week 4: Launch 2-3 primary formats
- Week 5-6: Secondary formats and continued social push
- Week 7-8: Continued email/organic promotion
- Ongoing: Monitor performance, optimize
