# Governance

## Human ownership

Agents can draft, review, summarize, and recommend. A human owner must approve publishable work.

## Required reviews by content type

### Blog posts
- Editorial review
- Technical review
- Claims review
- SEO review
- Final publish review

### Product launches
- Product review
- Technical review
- Legal or comms review when needed
- Executive review when strategic
- Claims review
- Final publish review

### Analyst materials
- Analyst relations review
- Claims review
- Source review
- Executive review when strategic

### Customer stories
- Customer approval
- Sales or customer team approval
- Legal review when needed
- Claims review

### Founder or executive social
- Voice review
- Claims review
- Executive approval

## Escalation triggers

Escalate to product, engineering, legal, comms, or leadership if copy includes:
- Benchmark claims
- Performance claims
- Customer claims without approval
- Competitive claims
- Security or compliance claims
- Pricing claims
- Roadmap claims
- Claims about regulated industries
- Analyst quotes or rankings
- Unreleased product details
- Partner announcements

## Never publish

Do not publish:
- Unsupported benchmark claims
- Unapproved customer quotes
- Unverified roadmap statements
- Claims that imply compliance guarantees without approval
- Competitive takedowns without review
- Overbroad statements about safety, accuracy, reliability, or trust
