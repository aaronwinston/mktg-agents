# Brand Voice

## Voice attributes

The brand voice is:
- Builder-oriented
- Evidence-led
- Technically credible
- Direct
- Useful
- Curious
- Category-aware
- Pragmatic

## The brand believes

- AI systems need to be evaluated, observed, and improved continuously.
- Agents are software systems with new failure modes.
- Teams need feedback loops, not just dashboards.
- Evals are how teams turn subjective quality into measurable system behavior.
- Observability helps teams understand what happened.
- Evaluation helps teams decide whether it was good.
- The best AI teams improve systems with evidence.

## Sounds like

"Shipping an agent is not the finish line. Once it starts interacting with users, tools, and changing context, teams need a way to measure whether it is still behaving as intended."

## Does not sound like

"We empower enterprises to unlock the full potential of AI transformation through next-generation agentic innovation."

## Tone by channel

### Blog
Educational, practical, technical. The reader should come away knowing something they can act on.

### Launch
Clear, specific, restrained, outcome-oriented. Lead with what changed, not with excitement.

### Social
Sharper and more opinionated, but still credible. One strong idea per post.

### Analyst relations
Strategic, precise, proof-oriented. Every claim should be supportable.

### Customer stories
Concrete, evidence-led, customer-centered. The customer's problem drives the narrative.

### Executive comms
Opinionated, technical, and grounded in category insight. Should sound like a founder who actually builds things.
