# Analyst response scenario

## Analyst or firm
A major AI infrastructure analyst firm published a market landscape report noting that Arize is a notable player in AI observability but raised questions about enterprise readiness, specifically around security, scalability, and integration depth with enterprise data stacks.

## Our position
Arize has enterprise customers in production. We have relevant security certifications. The integration ecosystem is deep and growing.

## Task
Prepare a briefing response for a follow-up conversation with the analyst.

## Key concerns to address
1. Enterprise security and compliance posture
2. Scalability at enterprise data volumes
3. Integration depth with enterprise data stacks

## Proof points available
- Enterprise customers (names require approval)
- Security certifications (to be confirmed with legal)
- Integration partners (can be named publicly)

## Claims requiring approval
- Any customer references require individual approval
- Security and compliance claims require legal review

## Desired outcome
The analyst updates their view to reflect enterprise readiness in the next report cycle.

## Required reviewers
AR lead, legal, executive

## Risks
Do not overclaim security guarantees. Do not name customers without approval. Do not speculate about roadmap.
